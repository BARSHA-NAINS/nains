​GET TO KNOW ME
I’m Barsha Haloi. Hobbyist Photographer based in Borjhar,Assam,India.

A 24 years old engineering graduate in Embedded System Design from CDAC Acts,Pune. Pursued engineering in Electronics and Telecommunication from Jorhat Institute of Science and Technology (JIST), Jorhat, Assam. Originally from Guwahati, completed schooling from Saint Claret School, Borjhar and HS(Sci) from Sai Vikash Junior College, Gotanagar.

A dream to put the World in a frame...

Why not grab your phone, step ahead and capture the beautiful life around you? That's what a girl like me desire. Being a hobbyist, I love to capture moments on a daily basis. With that curiosity and enthusiasm in my mind, I try to showcase my small little talent by my clicks and give the best shot I can. Photography gives me immense pleasure and makes me curious to do more. The clicks of mine holding the bright colours convey the story within. I believe, the picture you take with the camera is the imagination you want to create with reality. It's just an effort of mine to make people fall in love and develop a passion for photography. Hope you will be bliss by my work!
